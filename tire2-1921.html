About The Fuel Rebel

These Fuel Rebel wheels feature a Black finish and are sure to make your ride stand out! This particular wheel setup is in 18x9 with a -12 offset. The Fuel Rebel is a One Piece Alloy wheel that features exposed lugs. These beautiful 6 spoke wheels are available in a 6x5.5 configuration and will be sure to elevate the look of your vehicle build!

If you want to learn more about Fuel wheels or explore other models available, visit: Fuel Wheels.
